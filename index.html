F.A.Q

Full Payment is required upon ordering. This is due to the fact that if I begin making your order and cancel it, I can not reuse those materials as all my products are completely personalised.

In the event of a cancelled order, client shall be billed for all costs incurred up to the point of cancellation.

Production lead time is 3-5 days. If the items are not in stock it will take 10 days.
Personalised PJ's takes 5-10 days.
This will begin upon approval of final layout (custom orders) and confirmation of payment. Your order is not considered placed until your payment is received.
All items are made to order.

All items are subject to availability. If you choose an item that is not available, you will be notified immediately and will be offered other option.

We do not accept returns or offer refunds unless an item is faulty or damage. All sales are final and refunds will not be offered for change of mind.
We do not take responsibility for damage occured during postage as we take all necessary precautions to protect the product before shipping.

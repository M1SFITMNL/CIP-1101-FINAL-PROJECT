About Personalized by Jams

Looking for unique and personalized gifts that will make a lasting impression? Look no further than Personalised by Jams! Personalised by Jams is a small business that specializes in creating custom gifts that are perfect for any occasion. They offer a wide variety of products, including key rings, labels, cake toppers, mugs, chocolate bars, pajamas, tumblers, bottle sprays, guestbooks, party giveaways, gift sets, and cheese boards. And if you can't find exactly what you're looking for, they also offer custom design services to create a one-of-a-kind gift that your recipient will cherish.

Here's what makes Personalized by Jams stand out:

  • Unique and personalized gifts: Their products are a great way to show someone you care.
  • Wide variety of products: They have something for everyone on your list.
  • Custom design services: If you can't find what you're looking for, they can create it for you.
  • Perfect for any occasion: Their gifts are perfect for birthdays, holidays, weddings, or just to show someone you're thinking of them.

    • Our History

    Personalised by Jams isn't just a store, it's a story. It all started with the desire to create gifts that were more than just presents - they were meant to be cherished memories. The founders, fueled by a passion for personalization and a love for making people smile, poured their creativity into crafting unique items that could be tailored to every occasion and every loved one.

    Their journey began with a focus on heartfelt keepsakes like keyrings and cake toppers. As word spread about their dedication to quality and customization, their product line blossomed to encompass a delightful array of giftables - from mugs that warm the heart to chocolate bars that satisfy both taste buds and sentimental cravings.

    Today, Personalised by Jams remains a family-run business, where each item is crafted with the same care and attention to detail as those first special pieces. Their commitment to innovation ensures they're constantly expanding their offerings, from custom pajamas for cozy nights in to elegant cheese boards for unforgettable gatherings. But at the heart of it all, Personalised by Jams remains true to its roots.

    They believe in the power of personalization to transform an ordinary gift into something extraordinary. They believe in the joy of giving something that truly reflects the recipient's unique personality and the depth of your care.
